Root Collar Excavation

Safe, Precise Air Excavation for Tree Health Assessment

What is Root Collar Excavation?

Root collar excavation is a specialized air excavation service that carefully removes soil from around the base of a tree to expose the root collarâ€”the critical area where the trunk meets the root system. This non-invasive technique allows arborists to assess tree health, identify potential problems, and prevent decay without damaging delicate root structures.

Using pneumatic soil excavation technology, we can safely expose the root collar area to reveal hidden issues that may be compromising your tree's health and stability.

Benefits of Root Collar Excavation

  • Early Disease Detection: Identify decay, girdling roots, and other health issues before they become critical
  • Improved Tree Vitality: Exposing the root flare allows for better oxygen and water exchange, promoting healthier growth
  • Extended Tree Lifespan: Addressing root collar problems early can add decades to a tree's life
  • Safe, Non-Damaging Method: Air excavation preserves root integrity while removing soil
  • Proper Planting Depth Assessment: Determine if a tree was planted too deep and correct the issue
  • Prevention of Structural Failure: Identify stability issues before they lead to tree failure
  • Regulatory Compliance: Meet local tree preservation requirements for construction projects

Our Process

  1. Initial Assessment

    We evaluate the tree's condition, size, and surrounding environment to determine the scope of excavation needed.

  2. Site Preparation

    The work area is prepared and any nearby plants or structures are protected.

  3. Precision Air Excavation

    Using compressed air technology, we carefully remove soil from around the root collar without damaging roots or bark.

  4. Root Collar Inspection

    Once exposed, we thoroughly examine the root flare for girdling roots, decay, insects, or other issues.

  5. Documentation & Recommendations

    We photograph findings and provide detailed recommendations for any necessary treatments or corrective actions.

  6. Optional Soil Replacement

    If requested, we can replace soil to proper depth or leave the root collar exposed for improved tree health.

Ideal For

  • Trees showing signs of decline or stress
  • Trees with buried root flares
  • Pre-construction tree assessments
  • Heritage or high-value trees requiring preservation
  • Trees with suspected girdling roots
  • Post-construction tree evaluation
  • Landscape renovation projects
  • Tree health monitoring programs
